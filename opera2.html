Deepika Vusarthi Light Opera

The Yeomen of the Guard

Act II – Continued

[The conversation continues after a short pause.]

WILFRED

But tell me, Master Point, how shall I begin to practise this merry calling?

POINT

First, thou must learn to laugh at everything—at sorrow, at trouble, and most of all, at thyself!

WILFRED

It seems a strange profession, yet I will do my best to master it.
